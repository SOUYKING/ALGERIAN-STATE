üìñ Roleplay Terms & Rules

Essential terminology for quality roleplay (demo content).

Basic Terms

  • Role Play: playing a character in a fictional world
  • Meta Gaming: using OOC info in-game
  • Power Gaming: unrealistic actions (no fear/pain)
  • Bunny Jump: spamming jump to gain advantage

Important Rules

  • New Life Rule: after death you forget the event
  • Fairplay: no abusing mechanics
  • Pain RP: act injuries realistically
  • Mass RP: behave as if NPCs exist

Prohibited

  • Revenge Kill: kill after you died to the same person
  • Force RP: forcing without realistic chance
  • Stream Snipe: using stream info
  • Combat Log: leaving to avoid RP
